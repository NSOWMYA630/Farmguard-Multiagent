# Problem Statement

Climate change is increasing due to high carbon emissions from industries.

To offset emissions, companies purchase carbon credits.

However, small farmers face several problems:

• Farmers are unaware that trees on their land generate carbon credits.  
• Carbon auditing is expensive and complex.  
• Farmers do not have access to carbon credit markets.  
• Verification of carbon data is difficult and prone to fraud.

Because of these challenges, farmers lose a potential income source while industries struggle to find verified carbon credits.

FarmGuard AI solves this problem using AI, IoT, and a multi-agent system to automate carbon credit generation, verification, and marketplace access for farmers.
